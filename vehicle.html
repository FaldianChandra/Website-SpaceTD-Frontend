GALAXIA OVERVIEW

The GALAXIA spacecraft is billed as a fully reusable transport system designed to carry crew and cargo into the orbits on Earth, Moon, Mars and other Planets. The GALAXIA will be the most powerful launch vehicle the world has ever developed, capable of carrying up to 100 metric tons of fully reusable and 200 metric tons expendable.
